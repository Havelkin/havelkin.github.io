About

My skills begin with drawing and concepting, continue through ZBrush sculpting and Maya modeling — unwrapping and texturing in Mari, Photoshop or Substance Painter — and can be finalized with Arnold rendering, or PBR texturing and publishing in Unity 3D. I have experience in both the VFX and gaming industries. I specialize in characters and organic assets, but I'm not limited to that — props and environments are no problem either. I am very experienced in processing photogrammetry, from totally broken scans to clean, functional assets. With a passion for detail and strong hand-drawing skills, I'm able to deliver both photoreal and highly stylized work — and I enjoy both equally. I'm a creative person who loves coming up with original ideas and bringing them to life from scratch. Responsibility and reliability are a given. When it comes to games, I'm able — and I love — to build and tweak whole levels and sceneries.
